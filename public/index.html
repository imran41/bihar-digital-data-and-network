Got Questions?
Frequently Asked Questions
What qualifications do I need to join BDDN? ▼
No prior technical background is required for any of our courses. Whether you've just passed 12th grade, completed a BA/B.Com/B.Sc, or are a working professional looking to upskill — you are welcome. We start from the very basics in every program.
Are classes taught in Hindi or English? ▼
Classes are conducted in Hinglish (Hindi + English mix) to ensure maximum comfort and understanding. All reading materials and software interfaces are in English, which also helps students prepare for the professional environment.
Is placement assistance guaranteed? ▼
We offer placement assistance to all eligible graduates — this includes resume building, mock interview preparation, LinkedIn profile optimization, and direct referrals to our hiring partners. While we cannot guarantee a specific salary or company, our 95% placement track record speaks for itself. Students who complete all projects and attend placement sessions consistently get placed.
Can I attend a free demo class before enrolling? ▼
Absolutely! We encourage all prospective students to attend a free demo session before making any payment. This helps you understand our teaching style, meet the trainer, and make an informed decision. Call us at 9955889177 to schedule your free demo.
What certificate will I receive? ▼
Upon successful completion of the course (attendance + project submission), you will receive an official BDDN Course Completion Certificate. This certificate includes a QR code for online verification by employers. We also help you add your projects and certification to LinkedIn for maximum visibility.
